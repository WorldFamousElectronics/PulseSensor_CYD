Step 2: Wire Your PulseSensor

Connect to the CYD's P3 and CN1 connectors:

PulseSensor Wire CYD Pin
RED (+V) 3.3V (CN1)
BLACK (GND) GND (P3 or CN1)
PURPLE (Signal) GPIO 35 (P3)
⚠️ Use 3.3V, not 5V!
The CYD's CN1 provides 3.3V. The signal quality is best at this voltage.

Step 3: See Your Heartbeat

Place your finger on the PulseSensor. You'll see:

  • BPM — Your heart rate
  • Waveform — Scrolling pulse wave
  • IBI — Time between beats
  • RGB LED — Flashes red with each beat
